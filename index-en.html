Browser
How to find your browser version number

On a phone or tablet? No browser version required, just type in the name of the phone: iPhone 6S or Samsung Galaxy.

Internet Explorer & Firefox: use Help in menu bar, then About Internet Explorer or Alt-H-A (then Escape to exit)

It may open in a new tab.

Chrome:type "chrome:help" into the address bar (then BACK to exit) or use the menu (icon is 3 horizontal lines, in browser address bar), then About Google Chrome.

Safari: use About Safari in the Safari menu.

How to find software version

Find JAWS or NVDA version number

For JAWS, Press INSERT+J to make the JFW window active and then go to the menu bar. Choose the Help submenu and then the About option.

For NVDA, Right-click on your desktop and click the "NVIDIA Control Panel" option to open it. Alternately, double-click on the "NVIDIA Control Panel" icon in the system tray area of the taskbar. When the Welcome page opens, click on the Help menu and select "Updates.
